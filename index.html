Guidelines

  1. Begin play after set-up
  2. On each turn, the indicated player will get one dart
  3. Type the value of your throw into your box and hit next to input
    • A bullseye in the inner or outer ring is equal to 0
    • A double point value means you get another throw
    • A triple point value means you get two more throws and can choose the better of the two
  4. To complete a round, you must reach its peak value
    • Each round has a corresponding mountain peak from a continent (e.g. round one is Mt. Wilhelm at 14,000+ ft so you are aiming for a score of 14
  5. A throw less than the peak value is added to your tally and your turn is over
  6. If you overshoot the peak value, your tally returns to 0
    • On your next turn, you can get a throw that reaches the peak value and complete the round
    • Regardless of the tally total, a throw equal to the peak value advances you to the next round
    • The same is true of a bullseye in the inner or outer rings
  7. Completing all the peaks wins you the game!
